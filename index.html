2012 – President Obama’s re-election year saw a record $xxx in secret political spending (and that’s just the portion we know about).

The political disclosure is the most-requested rule from investors in SEC history (more than 1.2 million comments) [add cite] and is supported by 88% of both Democrats and Republicans.[cite mayday]

xx members of Congress have urged the FCC to take this action. But while President Obama’s appointee to the FCC, Tom Wheeler, took strong action to defend the open internet with Title II net neutrality, he has lacked the same resolve in fighting secret money.
